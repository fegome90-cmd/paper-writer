# Essay Outline Template

Use this scaffold before drafting.

## Question

- What exact question is the essay answering?

## Thesis

- What position will the essay defend?

## Roadmap Sentence

- Write one sentence that announces the thesis and the order of the major sections.

## Section Map

1. **Introduction**
   - Frame the problem
   - State the thesis
   - End with the roadmap
2. **Section 1 — Empirical Evidence**
   - What does the evidence show?
   - What are the limits of that evidence?
3. **Section 2 — Epistemic or Data Constraints**
   - What makes the evidence fragile, biased, incomplete, or context-bound?
4. **Section 3 — Governance / Regulation / Ethics**
   - What institutional controls or obligations shape legitimate use?
5. **Section 4 — Normative Synthesis**
   - What criteria, recommendations, or conditions follow from the analysis?
6. **Conclusion**
   - Answer the question directly
   - Re-state why the proposed conditions matter
