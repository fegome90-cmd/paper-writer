# Word Count Management

Track and enforce word counts per section to prevent scope creep and imbalance.

## Target Tracking

After the outline is approved, assign a word count target to each section.
Distribute the total target proportionally based on section importance.

| Section | Typical Share |
|---------|---------------|
| Introduction | 10-15% |
| Development sections | 60-70% (split among them) |
| Counterargument | 10-15% |
| Conclusion | 5-10% |

## Tolerance

Each section has a +/-10% tolerance band around its target.

- Within tolerance: proceed normally.
- Slightly over/under (within 15%): flag for adjustment during revision.
- Significantly over/under (beyond 15%): block progression until rebalanced.

## Escalation Protocol

When a section exceeds its tolerance:

1. **Identify cause**: Is the section overloaded with evidence? Missing a
   dimension? Repeating points made elsewhere?
2. **Rebalance options**: Trim, split into two sections, or redistribute word
   budget from a section that is under target.
3. **Re-run revision loop**: If trimming affects claims or evidence, follow
   `references/revision_loop.md` to re-categorize the change as structural,
   evidential, or prose.

## Whole-Essay Check

Before the final audit, verify the total word count is within +/-10% of the
assignment target. If not, scale sections proportionally rather than cutting
from a single section.
